Ottawa Chapter

The Ottawa chapter of GOSLING has both an online and face-to-face gathering place.

Face-toFace: Please go to the Meetup page for details about the time/location.

Online: This ottawa-gosling discussion list does not publish an archive, but we request new members introduce themselves. This is not a list for lurking. The list is an extension of the face-to-face meetings at the gaggles, and thus sometimes has messages that are aimed to the community but not specifically "on topic" towards the GOSLING goal.
